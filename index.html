Food Waste Management

Food waste management involves the processes and strategies aimed at minimizing, preventing, or reusing food waste. Some effective methods include composting, anaerobic digestion, and waste-to-energy technologies. Additionally, initiatives like food donation programs and consumer awareness campaigns can help reduce food waste..

Why is it necessary?

Food waste management is vital for conserving resources, reducing environmental pollution, addressing global hunger, and saving money for individuals, businesses, and governments.
